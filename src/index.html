Institution History

“Rafiuddin Memorial Academy” was established in the year 2020 in Madhaishur village of Boulotoli union of Munshiganj district’s Louhajong Upazila with the aim of playing a leading role in the field of education and spreading modern education at the marginal level. The activities of United Trust as a social welfare organization of the United Group are universally recognized for socio-economic and public welfare work. This institution is committed to provide modern, quality, effective and constructive education in a beautiful and aesthetic environment under the overall management of Bangladesh Digital Education Research Limited (bdeducation.org.bd) in collaboration with United Trust.
